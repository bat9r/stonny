## **Requirements to Win**

For your token to be eligible for the weekly $100K prize:

**Community Standards:**

*   Diversified holders
*   Active presence on Telegram and X (Twitter)
*   Contact information available for TON Foundation to reach you in bio

**Technical Standards:**

*   Majority of liquidity pool must be locked or renounced
*   No hidden functions in the smart contract
*   1% LP trading fee (recommended)

**Note:** Tokens attempting to game the system through fake holders or bot activity will be immediately disqualified.

## **How Winners Are Selected**

Every week, one token wins $100K based on transparent, on-chain performance.

Token selection is based on the mix of the following criteria:

*   Trading Volume
*   Market Cap
*   Liquidity
*   Holders

**Details:**

*   Each week one token will be selected based on a formula. The formula will be shared afterward.
*   Tokens can win max 3 times.
*   Tokens cannot win 2 times in a row.
*   Winner to be announced the following week.

Lower score = Higher ranking. All metrics come from on-chain data and are publicly verifiable.

**The Leaderboard:**

*   Updates every 5 minutes in real-time
*   New tokens appear after reaching 100+ holders and $10K liquidity.
*   Tracks all eligible tokens
*   Winner announced the following week after the Friday-to-Friday trading period

Track your ranking: [MemeRepublic Dashboard](https://memelandia.okhlopkov.com/)

## **What Winners Get**

Each week's winner receives $100K in two parts:

### **$50K Direct Purchases**

TON Foundation buys your token from the market:

*   Random timing to prevent front-running
*   Different wallet for each purchase (all publicly visible and net new)

### **$50K Liquidity Support**

After purchases are complete:

*   Added as liquidity to DEX pools
*   Stays in the market, creating lasting depth
*   Provided before next week's winner announcement

Everything is on-chain and trackable. No hidden moves, no surprises.
